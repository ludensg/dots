Room 4-308-D

Eye 2 - Graphite on paper, 3 x 3 inches. July, 2023.

This Wikipedia Eye records Room 4-308-D, in a certain student living community in Tallahassee. It documents the birthplace of the concept of the Wikipedia Eye.

This Eye relies solely on photographic documentation and the primary source that documents its existence (this website). Whether or not this Wikipedia Eye is still in exhibition is currently unknown.

Eye Placement and Commentary

This Eye was placed in a secret location inside the room the day prior to move-out day, July 31st, 2023.

"The thought process here was set in motion by the Buddhist concept of Samsara. The belief that life is a set of cycles, and as long as we aren't willing or able to transcend them, we are forever forced to wander in mundane existence.

To me, there is nothing that reflects this more than the cycle of occupancy of a student room in a college town. Every couple of months someone new inhabits this place, and the same things will happen for as long as the appartment exists. Rent will rise, the tenant will do the same mundane tasks as the one before, there will be breakups, there will be sleepless nights, and there will be days where they will sleep through their alarms, always doomed to worry about what comes next.

With this Eye, I can transcend just a little bit. Even if this Eye is not found, for as long as it inhabits this place, it will be a presence in the lives of the people that come next.

And even if it's already gone, just by placing this Eye here and recording its existence, I have already forged a more enduring connection to this room, and the people that will inhabit it next.

Now, when other people find out about the existence of this Eye (and what it saw), it's as if they are looking through it, back in time.

- Daniel Gandelman, when asked about the meaning of this particular Wikipedia Eye
